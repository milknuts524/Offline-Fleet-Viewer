Privacy Policy

Teitoku Techou does not collect, transmit, or share personal information.

All game-related data used by the app is imported manually by the user and stored locally on the device.

The app:

does not require account registration
does not connect to external game servers
does not perform automated gameplay
does not upload user data
does not include analytics or advertising SDKs

Imported JSON files are used only for local viewing within the app.

This app is an unofficial personal companion viewer and is not affiliated with DMM, C2 Architecture, or related rights holders.
